How to Play

1. An item appears on the conveyor.

2. Click the correct bin: Landfill or Recycle.

3. +10 points for correct sorting; -5 for mistakes.

4. Fill the progress bar to 100% to finish the level.

5. Items move faster as you progress!

6. Levels unlock in sequence once completed.
